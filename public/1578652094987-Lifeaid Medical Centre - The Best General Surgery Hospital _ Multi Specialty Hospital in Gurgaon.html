Lifeaid Medical Centre

We want to become your friend when it comes to health advice.

Listed amongst the top hospitals in Gurgaon, Lifeaid Medical Centre takes pride when it comes to serving your loved ones with advanced treatment and care. We want to become your friend when it comes to health advice.

Lifeaid Medical Centre is a one-stop solution for your entire family’s health issues. We specialize in minimal invasive surgeries i.e surgeries performed by various Endoscopes and that makes it stand as the best general surgery hospital in Gurgaon. We have a team of leading doctors with experience in their own fields such as General Surgery, Laparoscopic Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Infertility Management, Paediatrics, Paediatric Surgery, Orthopaedic Surgery with Joint Replacement, Urology & Urosurgery, Cancer Surgery and Cancer Management, Cosmetic & Plastic Surgery, Neurosurgery and Neurology, Spine Surgery, ENT, Cardiology,  Medicine, Physiotherapy, Dental care and many others, that makes our hospital amongst the leading hospitals in Gurgaon.

MODERN MEDICAL FACILITIES

Lifeaid Medical Centre is spread over 12000 square feet covered area and is elegantly designed to suit the tastes of the highly demanding patients of the millennium city. The multi-storied hospital has OPD Services, Diagnostics(lab, ultrasound & digital X-Ray), Two Fully Equipped Operation Theatres, Labour Room, Patient`s lift & State of the art Patient Rooms which offers a comfortable stay at the hospital , and make the procedure of getting treated enjoyable. We are amongst the most affordable hospital in Gurgaon, offering the latest treatments at competitive prices.
